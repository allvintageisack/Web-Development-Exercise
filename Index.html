Tamuwa.com

This project involved the development of a professional and informative website for Tamuwa, Kenya's largest biomass briquette company. The website effectively showcases Tamuwa's commitment to sustainable energy by highlighting their process of converting agricultural biomass waste into high-quality energy briquettes and pellets. It features detailed information about their products, the environmental benefits of their solutions, and their contribution to a sustainable future. The site design emphasizes their eco-friendly mission while providing a clear and engaging user experience for visitors interested in renewable energy solutions.
